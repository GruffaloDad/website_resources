Professional results without compromise

For a document to stand out, the language it contains needs to be perfect, nothing less will do.

This is why I endeavour to make each text in English that extra bit special. To this end, every word is checked not once but multiple times to ensure it meets my rigorous standards. The register of the language for the intended audience is always correct, and I embellish within limits to enrich the content for readers, whether native speakers or otherwise.

This website details three of my spheres of involvement:

In proofreading and translation (into English), I aim to bring out the best in the work to obtain the greatest reach.

Social networking is no less of an art, in that one has to attract followers and maintain their interest; any such engagement is determined by the post and the accuracy of the language in which it is delivered.

Slogan and lyric creation both require great thought and deliberation. An intense period is spent developing numerous versions and carrying out revisions before there is any sense of arriving at a satisfactory conclusion.

Act now. Send me an email to discuss your requirements.

Proofreading/editing/copywriting

As a proofreader/editor/copywriter, my regular clients include translators and universities, hence I correct science papers intended for publication on a gamut of subjects, as well as legal texts and contracts. In addition, I have corrected and edited three academic books (and counting), content for websites, corporate brochures and marketing materials. Each has posed its own challenges, e.g. maintaining a historical register, explaining complex concepts in an approachable manner or grabbing the attention of the reader.


Contact me for further information on my proofreading/editing/copyrighting services.

Creative writing - slogans and lyrics

A good marketing slogan in English has to be at once concise and communicative. The process of arriving at suitable candidates is one of creating, revising and accepting or rejecting ideas.

Let me help you arrive at the best solution for your project.

Lyric creation is a fun yet highly challenging activity, where the words, tune and rhythm have to combine seamlessly.

To date I have interpreted or written verses for traditional folk music and modern religious songs, although I would welcome the opportunity to develop lyrics for any musical style.

This is definitely an area where sensitivity is required. Perhaps you are a songwriter and want to keep total control over the creative process. If so, I could act act as a consultant, providing ideas and alternative phrasings.

Science papers

I have proofread hundreds in my on with a staggering array of subjects – animal-assisted therapy, biodegrading waste water in treatment plants, cosmetic procedures, retrieving collagen from the feet of chicken, facets of the calls of gibbons, treating waste from leather and plastics, using polymers to delivery medication… The list goes on and on. The likelihood is I will have done something akin to your science paper at some point in time, so do not hesitate to get in touch with me.

Websites

The language on websites is important and acts as a direct reflection of our businesses and ourselves. Clients are easily swayed, and seeing inaccuracies in wording on a web page might just put them off contacting you. Whatever the website, drop me a line and we can discuss your project. Let me help you in conveying your message perfectly.

Publications

There is nothing quite like seeing your words in print, and I have been fortunate to correct and edit several books published digitally or in paper form. I check every single sentence multiple times, often rephrasing it in the context of the paragraph and the register of the text until I am perfectly happy with the result. I feel this is the only approach to be taken to ensure the quality of the end result.
