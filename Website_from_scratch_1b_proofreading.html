PROOFREADING / TRANSLATIONS

As a proofreader/editor/copywriter, my regular clients include translators and universities, hence I correct science papers intended for publication on a gamut of subjects, as well as legal texts and contracts. In addition, I have corrected and edited three academic books (and counting), content for websites, corporate brochures and marketing materials. Each has posed its own challenges, e.g. maintaining a historical register, explaining complex concepts in an approachable manner or grabbing the attention of the reader, and the challenge of penning a smart little slogan is something I relish.
