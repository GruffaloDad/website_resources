For a document to stand out the language it contains needs to be perfect, nothing less will do.

This is why I endeavour to make each text in English that extra bit special. To this end, every word is checked not once but multiple times to ensure it meets my rigorous standards. The register of the language for the intended audience is always correct, and I embellish within limits to enrich the content for readers, whether native speakers or otherwise.

This website details three of my spheres of involvement. In proofreading and translation (into English), I aim to bring out the best in the work to obtain the greatest reach. Social networking is no less of an art, in that one has to attract followers and maintain their interest; any such engagement is determined by the post and the accuracy of the language in which it is delivered. Slogan and lyric creation both require great thought and deliberation. An intense period is spent developing numerous versions and carrying out revisions before there is any sense of arriving at a satisfactory conclusion.
